About Yeji

Yeji Kim is a Korean American classical violinist. She began performing on the international stage at the age of 8 and since then, has performed in Prague, Salzburg, Shanghai, Beijing, Tokyo and Chicago among others. Yeji has attended the Aspen Music Festival, Paciﬁc Music Festival and Meadowmount School of Music.

She completed her Artist Diploma at the San Francisco Conservatory and holds a Master's in violin performance from the Peabody Conservatory, where she studied with Keng-Yuen Tseng. She graduated in 2019 with a double degree from Johns Hopkins University and Peabody Conservatory in violin performance and cognitive science.

She has also performed in masterclasses with artists such as Midori, Donald Weilerstein and Joel Smirnoﬀ, as well as the Paciﬁca and Chiara quartets.
